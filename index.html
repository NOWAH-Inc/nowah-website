The Reality

Managing a clinic should not be this challenging.

Physicians across Ontario rely on disconnected systems, frequent workarounds, and software not designed for real-world care delivery.

Fragmented Records

Patient data is spread across multiple systems that do not communicate. Clinicians spend valuable time assembling information rather than focusing on patient care.

Administrative Burden

For every hour spent on patient care, physicians spend nearly two hours on paperwork. The system is designed for documentation, billing, and referrals rather than patient needs.

Outdated Technology

Most clinic software was developed decades ago and has only been updated incrementally. As a result, these systems are slow, unintuitive, and do not align with current healthcare practices.

Our Story

Named after the person who inspired its creation.

NOWAH was created in response to personal loss and frustration with a healthcare system that does not meet the needs of those it serves. We have experienced these challenges firsthand as patients, family members, and individuals navigating a system that often complicates difficult moments.

We chose to enter this field intentionally. We are building NOWAH because we believe healthcare technology should serve the people within the system, not the other way around.

"Noah, this one is for you. Every bit of it."
